# Aesthetic Direction

## Desired product feeling

Premium operational SaaS, calm, dense but legible, trustworthy.

## Visual principles

- Strong IA over decoration.
- Restrained color usage.
- Clear decision hierarchy.

## Information density

Operational dense

## Color posture

Neutral base, one accent, semantic state colors only.

## Typography posture

System sans, strong headings, readable metadata.

## Layout posture

Three-zone workflow: navigation, work surface, evidence/decision.

## Motion posture

Subtle transitions only.

## Copy / microcopy voice

Direct, operational, no marketing fluff.

## Must feel like

A mature tool used daily by operators.

## Must not feel like

A generic demo admin template.
