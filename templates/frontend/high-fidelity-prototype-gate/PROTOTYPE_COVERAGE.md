## Completeness notes

- Every listed surface/interaction must be complete: status must be `COMPLETE`, `PASS`, or `DONE`.
- Local prototype artifacts, screenshots, and evidence paths must exist under the workflow directory; HTTP(S) prototype URLs are allowed for artifact URLs only.
- Missing, draft, partial, blocked, or placeholder rows fail the gate. Do not list core journeys until they are represented by a real prototype artifact and screenshot/evidence.
- A prototype is not complete if it only shows a landing/hero screen, static tabs, or isolated cards without the core workflow path.
